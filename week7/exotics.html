Exotics

  • Exotics

    • All exotics kennels are based on species, size, and basic needs of your pet. All exotics kennels will include basic water, food, and heat/light requirements for the specific species.

We care for various exotics including many reptilian and mammal species such as:

Hamsters Rabbits Chinchillas Rats and Mice
Chameleons Snakes Iguanas Turtles

* There are many more species welcome at Pet Purrfect Kennels. Please inquire with our friendly office staff regarding any other breeds or specific questions.

* For safety reasons, we cannot care for any poisonous species.
