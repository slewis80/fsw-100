Birds

  • Aviary

    • All aviary kennels are based on the specific size and breed of your feathered friend. For friendly, vegetarian birds there is the option to have periods of "free-fly" time, where they are allowed out of their cage for short periods during cage cleaning.

Some types of birds that we care for here at Pet Purrfect Kennels are:

Parakeets Parrots Love Birds
Cockatoos Cockatiels Finches
Conures Macaws Poicephalus

* There are many other birds we can care for while you're away... please inquire with our friendly office staff regarding any other breeds or specific questions.

* All aviary kennels will include basic water, food, and heat/light requirements for the specific type of bird.
