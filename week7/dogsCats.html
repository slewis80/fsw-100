Dog and Cat Kennels

For the comfort of both species, the dog and cat sections of our facility are on opposite sides of the building, and the dog area has been specially sound-proofed.

There are several "levels of luxery" to choose from:

We can also accomodate pet "families", where multiple pets are kenneled together in a single, larger kennel. Depending on pet size, this may be a larger kennel for your chosen "luxery level" or it may be the highest "luxery level" in order to meet our strict standards of pet to kennel size ratio.

  • Basic Kennel

    • Your basic needs kennel, appropriately sized to fit your pet(s).

  • Queen Size Kennel

    • A slightly roomier kennel, with extra species/breed specific toys.

  • King Size Kennel

    • The roomiest kennel available for your pet(s) size, with extra toys, larger/comfier beds, and ammenities such as PetTV.

* All kennels, regardless of size, will include 24 hour access to water, your pet(s) specific food given on a shedule of your choosing, and an appropriately sized bed.

* Both dogs and cats will have the option to participate in "play-time" (dogs) or "free-roam time" (cats). Please specify if your pet(s) do not get along with others or has social anxiety.
